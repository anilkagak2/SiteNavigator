ABOUT ME

Mike Wazowski

Michael "Mike" Wazowski is the deuteragonist of the Monsters, Inc. franchise and the titular protagonist of Mike's New Car. He is Sulley's best friend, roommate, and assistant.

Mike is constantly thought to be the main protagonist of Monsters University. He is voiced by Billy Crystal, by Carlos Alazraqui in the first video game and Monsters, Inc. Scream Arena, by Danny Burstein in SW 2.5 (The Pitch Wars), and by Ivar Brogger in Cars Mater-National.
